What is the PAES?

The PAES (Prueba de Acceso a la Educación Superior) is a standardized test in Chile designed to assess the knowledge and skills of students seeking to enter university. It evaluates subjects like language, mathematics, and sciences.

This section provides a selection of essays and practice materials to help students prepare for the PAES.
