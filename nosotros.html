About Us

Click and Read was created by two passionate individuals, Benjamin Poblete and Tabatha Cubillos. The idea came from a common problem at our school library, where there weren't enough books for everyone, and the limited reading time made it difficult for students to enjoy their readings. We wanted to solve this issue by creating a digital platform where students could access books anytime and anywhere, promoting reading across our school. Our mission is to inspire learning, assist with academic work, and offer a space for creative exploration. With the addition of our innovative "PAES" section, we aim to encourage student autonomy and help with exam preparation in a more efficient and engaging way.

We are excited to continue fostering a love for reading and learning through this digital space. Together, we believe we can make a difference in the way we approach education and literature in our community.Our page was created mainly to help the Zapallar high school, BT Associates seeks to satisfy needs that others seek to do and help them along the way, this page was financed by Hector Ceballos, English teacher at the Zapallar high school
